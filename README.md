## Iteration 1

### Assignment Requirements:
1.Record your weekly meetings for Iteration 1 – use the Group Meeting Report template.
Make sure you include (meeting agenda, date, meeting actions, attendees, and
information about the next meeting).
2. Complete the Stakeholder Register. You can use the template provided or create your
own. On the register, describe your stakeholders using a list with a role assigned for
each in the website development (since no specialist knowledge is required, it is
expected that each group member covers each role at some point during the project).
3. Describe at least three (3) different personas (fictional characters) that will use your e-
commerce website.
4. As a Group, review the three (3) personas. You may wish to include first and final
impressions as a user and whether they are relevant.
